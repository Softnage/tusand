Our Services

Explore Our Services

Room Service

Whether you're craving a hearty breakfast in bed, a midday snack, or a romantic dinner for two, our room service menu has a wide range of delicious options to choose from. You can place your order easily through our website or by calling our front desk, and our friendly staff will deliver your meal right to your door. So sit back, relax, and let us take care of your culinary needs during your stay with us.

Laundry Service

We also offer convenient laundry services for our guests. Whether you need a fresh set of clothes for a business meeting or simply want to refresh your wardrobe during your stay, our laundry service is here to help. You can schedule a pickup and drop-off time that works best for you, and we'll take care of the rest.

Conference Service

Our hotel also offers comprehensive conference services to meet your business needs. We have a range of modern meeting rooms and state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment to ensure that your presentations are effective and engaging. Our team can also assist with catering, transportation, and other logistics to make sure that your event runs smoothly.

Special Meal Arrangement

We offer special meal arrangements to meet our guests' unique dietary needs and preferences, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, low-fat, and allergy-friendly options. Our culinary team prepares customized menus using fresh, high-quality ingredients. To arrange a special meal, guests can inform us when making a reservation or contacting the front desk during their stay. We strive to ensure that our guests' dining experience is enjoyable and hassle-free.

Tusand Hotel

Tusand Hotel is situated in Kumasi, Ghana. The hotel boasts 24-hour electricity and air-conditioning. Parking on site is available.
