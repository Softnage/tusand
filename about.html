Tusand Hotel

Welcome to Tusand Hotel

Tusand Hotel is situated in Kumasi, Ghana. The hotel boasts 24-hour electricity and air conditioning. Parking on-site is available.

The rooms are well-spaced and designed with en suite bathrooms. Two of the room types are fitted with air conditioning. Each room features a non-flat screen TV with satellite/cable services, a desk, a chair and a telephone. Toiletries are complimentary.

Breakfast is served daily for a surcharge. Local and continental dishes can be ordered at the on-site restaurant while a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks can be ordered at the well-stocked bar. 24-Hour room service is also provided by the hotel.

Tusand Hotel is a 10-minute drive from Ejisu township.
