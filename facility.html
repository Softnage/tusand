Our Facilities

Explore Our Facilities

Conference Room

Our hotel offers modern and well-equipped conference rooms for business meetings, conferences, and events. Our conference rooms are designed to cater to various group sizes and are equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment to ensure that your presentations are effective and engaging. Our expert team is also available to provide catering and logistics assistance to ensure that your event runs smoothly. Contact us today to learn more about our conference room facility and how we can help make your event a success.

Bar & Restaurant

Our hotel features a stylish and comfortable bar and restaurant where you can relax and enjoy delicious meals and drinks. Our restaurant offers a range of delectable dishes made from fresh and locally sourced ingredients, while our bar offers a variety of beverages, including cocktails, wine, and beer. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a romantic dinner, our bar and restaurant are the perfect spots to satisfy your cravings. Our friendly staff is always on hand to ensure that you have a delightful dining experience.

Free Parking

We offer complimentary on-site parking for our guests, making it easy and convenient for you to park your car during your stay. Our parking lot is secure and well-lit, giving you peace of mind knowing that your vehicle is safe and secure. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, our free parking facility is one less thing to worry about during your stay with us.

Recreational Center

Our hotel features a recreational center where you can unwind and relax during your stay. Our friendly staff is always on hand to assist you and ensure that you have a delightful experience at our recreational center.

Tusand Hotel

Tusand Hotel is situated in Kumasi, Ghana. The hotel boasts 24-hour electricity and air-conditioning. Parking on site is available.
